Earthquake in Japan - 2011

The earth rocked and the waves rose high,
The fleeing people had no time to sigh.
Such earthquake was faced by Japan,
Which indicated nature hasn't got any ban;
Bridges and buildings came crashing down
Due to the water with lava brown.
The water destroyed rails and highways.
T'will take long time to rebuild ,prime minister says,
The country known for many an earthquake,
This time had no measures to take.
Many properties were destroyed and lives were lost,
And to government, it was great cost.
The nuclear reactor burst with bang,
On the roofs ,helpless people clang.
"No such disaster again",to God I pray,
Which would make buildings heave and sway.
 Parva Patel
