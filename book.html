Parva's poems

Books

Oh,you are the best source of knowledge book,
You are hanging on the last hook.
The one who took,
You from the last nook,
Would get more and more knowledge;
without any distraction or wedge.
Your words like river flow,
Sometimes high,sometimes low;
The rythm your words sing,
Can make anyone a king.
Oh,and your pictures are very fine
Like sweet fruits of pine.
 Parva Patel
