Trees

The trees,you are,swaying in the wind,
Oh,and you are very selfless and kind;
You give us food and shelter;
So that no one runs helter-skelter;
The wood given by your trunk,
Is very useful in making the bunk.
Your branches sometimes are covered with thorns,
Sometimes they give us delicious corns.
Your juicy fruits hang high,
That we can't jump so high with our thigh.
And your food preparing leaf,
Is your greatest belief.
And fruits your flower bear,
Are sometimes common,sometimes rare.
 Parva Patel
