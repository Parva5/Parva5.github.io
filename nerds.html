A nerd speaks

Nerds is what we have been tagged
Due to academic prizes we have bagged.
You say, we haven't got sense of dress
But unlike you, we seldom undergo stress.
"Syllabus is not complete, exams to hell!
Oh God, we are in a stressful shell."
Are your words before every exam starts.
But during exams, you cannot resist using our carts
We help you in the exams by letting you copy
And prevent your life from getting sloppy.
All of you may flutter in your high esteem
But remember, it was us who gave you the steam.
You ridicule us for studying all day
And so try to distract us from our way.
But do mind that it is all
Worthless as a cow in a mall.
Parva Patel
