Grammar

Grammar,Grammar,Grammar....
It's a wonderful name!
Grammar,Grammar,Grammar....
It has a wide-spread fame.

There are many of its kinds,
But the most famous is nouns;
You must be knowing its value
It's worth a thousand ounce!

The other main type
Of Grammar is verbs,
It is known to be its heartbeat
And fierce as cubs.

Oh,the noun describing words
Are very well known as adjectives.
They add meaning to sentence
Like blasting,terrific explosives.

Let us study some examples
As noun's example is weak
I also have one other word
And that is duck's beak.

Now,let's have some words
Describing noun as for strength
We also have many words
Another example is length.

The sentence which is part of it
Has got parts like subject.
But it also has another part
Yes,and that part is object.

Grammar,you will be very useful
Till we pass the twelfth grade.
But you will stay forever with us
To you,we will not bade.
 Parva Patel
