2008 Financial crisis

The markets went down, down, down
Powerful banks worried about their crown;
For it was a crisis perpetrated by them,
Which got the world in this jam.
Housing loans, both prime and sub-prime
Issued freely for quite a long time.
Complex products based on these loans,
Rendered worthless like a pile of stones.
For defaults will happen if you give
Irresponsible loans to create assets new
And banks sold them to transfer the risk
This cycle continued at pace brisk.
Till one day, when this bubble burst
There started the race to become the first;
To remove these toxic assets from the books.
Lured by money, they acted like crooks.
Multi million dollar bonuses for these snobs
Even though millions around the world lost their jobs.
The public anger against them surged,
As Lehman failed and Merrill merged
Money, O money, so much greed you can brew
Harming the many, benefiting the few.
Parva Patel
