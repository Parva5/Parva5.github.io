After exams are over

After exams are over,
We get rid of our studies.
Just sing and dance and enjoy;
And play with your buddies.

I will go into malls and shops
And big big restaurants.
I will take with me my parents
And also uncle and aunt.

No pencil,no paper in hand,
Only we've to do is play,play,play.
We can play with bat and ball.
And toys and also clay.

We get a long summer, vacation,
For a long ,long time.
We eat mangoes and watermelons
Muskmelons and lime.

There are many,more things to do.
After the exams are over,
We have time to see the passing cars,
And aircrafts that hover.

But most important of all,
Is that we overcome tension,
We also drive away things like
Books,pens and depression.

But after the vacation is over,
We start a new year.
We have to welcome back the books
As exams will come nearer.
 Parva Patel
