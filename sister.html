Sister

You are very useful,oh sister,
To brother,you are as king's minister.

You tie me a wonderful rakhi.
Of colours red,orange or khakhi.

Of course,you are a bit small,
But you attend to my every call.

You always give me advice on right,
Be it be day or night.

And you are very beautiful,
As well as very useful.
 Parva Patel
