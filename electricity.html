Electricity

Electricity, in our daily life,
Is as useful as spoon and knife;
It lights up things and makes us feel nice,
From night to morning, till we arise.
Generated by thermal coal,
Of our lives, it seldom takes toll.
The tubelights, coolers and the fans,
And ACs as well as the electric pans,
Are few devices which run by it;
It also makes houses and roads lit.
From roadside beggars to wealthy man,
To live without it, one hardly can.
It is source of power for many things;
Like a Sun for the realm of non-livings.
But nowadays, it is wasted so much
Power cuts have to be imposed, condition is such.
So let us make its economical use,
And about its wastage, we prevent ourselves to muse.
 Parva Patel
