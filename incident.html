An incident

This was an incident,when I was three,
At the time when I was usually free.
At that time I was also very naughty
As I was naughty,so was haughty.
So I went to buy clothes with my mother
But I seemed to have attention on some other.
Yes,it was an ugly girl in the shop,
To whom I felt like giving a chop.
It looked like she was four,
But she wanted many, much, more.
So at the mirror I dashed her head,
In her unconciousness,she wade.
But as she did not cry,
I dashed her head for the second try.
And over her head,emptied a bottle of powder;
But as it had gone beyond the border,
So I decided to run away,
As I had to go a long way.
Afterwards I thought she must have cried
For I had been succesful in second try I tried!
Parva Patel
