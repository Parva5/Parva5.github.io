My room

Like a complete mess is my room
Even though the servant sweeps through the broom.
In the corner of my room is my study table
On which there are many books with label.
Beside the study table,is my cupboard
Where I have my childhood toys hoard.
Also there are beds,in number of twos
Accompanied by small cupboard,where I keep my books.
On side of cupboard,facing my table
Stuck there is a large wallpaper.
Also on my table I have a small clock
On the door of my cupboard is the key hanging to lock.
There is a beautiful bedsheet spread,
On each of my two beds.
And I like relaxing in such a room,
Where there is no entry for doom!
 Parva Patel
