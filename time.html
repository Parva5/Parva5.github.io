Parva's poems

I like that time

When in the morning rises the sun,
And I have my bun;
When the day becomes hot
Everybody sleep on their coat.
I feel like singing in the mike
As this is the time I like.

When I see a farmer in the day
Going to the field on his way.
To make the grains sprout
On their way to out.
I feel like singing in the mike
As this is the time I like.

While cows in the shed bellow
While eating the hay in yellow;
And when I see a woman in red,
Stitching with the needle and thread;
I feel like singing in the mike
As this is the time I like.
Parva Patel
