Prayer for my home

Home ,my sweet home,
At its enterance ,there is a beautiful dome.
As for prayer ,I'd like to pray,
For love and peace to always stay.
And also joy, merriment and gay;
Happiness and flowers of may.
I want some things out of my house,
That is sorrow and timid ,scrawling mouse.
Also storm ,cyclone ,rain and wind,
And evil spirits ,you'd better mind!
This is what for my house I pray,
For good things to always stay.
 Parva Patel
