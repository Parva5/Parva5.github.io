Parva's poems

Nature

Oh,you are so beautiful,nature!
You never do the torture;
Everyone in front of you knelt
Because you give leather for belt.
Your trees give us wood;
Which is helpful in making our hood.
You give us iron which is formed into bars,
It is very useful for making the cars.
You let anyone make noise
As you have no other choice.
The hive made by humming bees,
On your lushy,green trees,
On your land,there is someone's house,
It can be timid, scrawling mouse.
Some of your grass is very rare,
Which is grazed by white mare.
And you are too bold;
To give us freely the shining gold.
The small children like to leap,
In your leaves' heep.
When it rains,
We get delicious cairns.
Your colourful stars glitter;
And make everyone come out of their shelter.
Many times you are a wonderful sight,
And that scene is marvellous and right.
There are many kinds of your animals,
Some are avians,some are reptiles,some are mammals.
In the daylight soars high the kite,
Wheares owl toots at night.
Oh,nature,you are so......colourful!
And along with flowers,you are very beautiful!
Parva Patel
